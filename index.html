The Band

NightTimeDrive

Born from the glow of Route 66 and rooted in the Southwest, NightTimeDrive creates Chicano Pop — soulful, instrument-forward alternative pop shaped by culture, atmosphere, and cinematic storytelling.

Built on a foundation of soul, the music blends warm musicianship with deliberate songwriting and layered, intentional production. The result carries the feeling of late-night drives through the city — reflective, expansive, and always moving forward.

"Built for open windows, late exits, and the drive you don't want to end."

NightTimeDrive operates as a focused creative collective, anchored by a consistent core while collaborating with select musicians and artists chosen to best serve each record. In doing so, the project continues to shape the sound and identity of Chicano Pop as a modern cultural voice.
